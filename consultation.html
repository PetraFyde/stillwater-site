Initial Consultation

Assessment and planning for NeuroRegulation Hypnotherapy

What the Consultation Is

The initial consultation is a focused assessment session. Its purpose is to understand how your nervous system is responding to your symptoms and determine whether NeuroRegulation Hypnotherapy is an appropriate fit.

This session is structured and practical. It is not a full hypnotherapy session and does not require emotional reliving.

What We Cover

  • The nature and duration of your symptoms
  • Nervous system activation and stress response patterns
  • How tinnitus or relational stress is currently affecting daily life
  • Previous treatments or coping strategies
  • Goals and readiness for hypnotherapy work

Outcome of the Session

By the end of the consultation, you will have clarity about:

  • Whether this approach is appropriate for your situation
  • What type of sessions would be recommended
  • Expected focus and structure of the work
