Tinnitus Habituation & Emotional Regulation Program

This program supports individuals living with persistent tinnitus by focusing on habituation, emotional regulation, and nervous system balance.

Program Overview

An 8-session structured hypnotherapy approach designed to reduce distress, calm nervous system reactivity, and support quality of life alongside audiological care.

Key Focus Areas

  • Emotion regulation around tinnitus perception
  • Reduction of stress reactivity and sleep disturbance
  • Integration alongside hearing care

Program Structure & Pricing

Eight sessions over 8 to 12 weeks. Total investment: $1,800. Financing options available through CareCredit and Cherry.
