Tinnitus & Nervous System Regulation

Tinnitus is strongly influenced by nervous system arousal. When the brain remains in a threat-monitoring state, sound perception becomes amplified and difficult to ignore.

NeuroRegulation Hypnotherapy supports tinnitus habituation by reducing autonomic arousal and interrupting the feedback loop between stress and sound awareness.

This work does not promise to eliminate sound. The goal is to reduce distress, reactivity, and the intrusive quality of tinnitus so it no longer dominates attention or daily life.

Relational Stress & Emotional Dysregulation

Relationship loss can destabilize the nervous system, especially when attachment bonds are disrupted suddenly or without closure. This often results in emotional looping, intrusive thoughts, anxiety, and difficulty moving forward.

NeuroRegulation Hypnotherapy addresses the physiological stress response underlying these symptoms. Sessions focus on emotional stabilization, attachment deactivation, and restoring internal regulation.
