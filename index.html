Regulating the Nervous System After Prolonged Stress

NeuroRegulation Hypnotherapy works at the level of the nervous system rather than treating symptoms in isolation. This approach supports individuals whose systems have become locked in heightened alert, emotional overwhelm, or sensory reactivity following prolonged stress or loss.

Who This Work Is For

NeuroRegulation Hypnotherapy supports nervous system regulation in cases of:

  • Relationship loss and attachment-related stress
  • Tinnitus and sensory reactivity
  • Chronic stress and hyperarousal states
  • Sleep disruption linked to nervous system activation
  • Sensory sensitivity and ongoing hypervigilance

Why Relationship Loss and Tinnitus?

Although relationship loss and tinnitus may appear unrelated, both are driven by the same underlying nervous system processes. In each case, the brain becomes locked in a heightened threat and vigilance state, leading to emotional distress, sensory amplification, and difficulty settling. NeuroRegulation Hypnotherapy focuses on stabilizing these core response patterns rather than treating symptoms in isolation. By working at the level of nervous system regulation, this approach can support recovery across different presentations that share the same biological mechanisms.
