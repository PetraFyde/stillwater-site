Relationship Loss Recovery Program

About the Program

This is a 6-session program designed to support emotional regulation, attachment recalibration, and nervous system stabilization after a breakup or significant separation.

What We Focus On

  • Sleep support and emotional stabilization
  • Identity reinforcement after loss
