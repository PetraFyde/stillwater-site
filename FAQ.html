FAQs & Payment Policies

Is this therapy?

No. NeuroRegulation Hypnotherapy is not psychotherapy and does not diagnose or treat mental illness. It is a non-medical service focused on nervous system regulation and symptom reduction.

Do you take insurance?

Services are private pay and not billed to insurance.

What payment options are available?

Services may be paid in full or through a short-term structured payment plan. For payment plans, 40% is required at booking, with the remaining balance automatically processed over eight weeks.

What happens if a payment fails?

Missed or failed payments pause services until resolved. All payment plans require a valid payment method on file.

Is hypnotherapy safe?

Hypnotherapy is a guided, focused state of attention. Clients remain aware and in control throughout the session.

Can this be used alongside medical care?

Yes. This work may be used alongside medical or mental health treatment, but it does not replace professional medical care.
